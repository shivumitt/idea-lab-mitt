What is AICTE IDEA Lab?

The All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) launched the IDEA Lab scheme in January 2021 to establish innovation hubs in approved engineering institutions. Co-funded by AICTE (50%) and the institution (50%), each lab receives up to ₹90L to build a cutting-edge fabrication and design ecosystem aligned with NEP 2020.

Hands-on Learning
Students get real-world experience through ideation, prototyping, and product development — bridging classroom theory with practical application.
Interdisciplinary Collaboration
Open to all departments — engineering, science, and management — encouraging cross-pollination of ideas and diverse team building.
21st Century Skills
Critical thinking, design thinking, problem solving, communication, and lifelong learning — skills demanded by modern industry and global innovation.
IP & Entrepreneurship
Support for patents, copyrights, incubation, and startup development. Students and faculty are guided from idea to commercialization.
Industry Connect
Outreach to MSMEs, startups, and industries for consultancy, testing services, and resource sharing under the "Make in India" initiative.
Societal Impact
IDEA Lab addresses community needs through NGO partnerships and focuses on food, agriculture, healthcare, and smart city solutions.
Vision & Mission
VISION

Develop commercially viable products that address socio-economic needs through entrepreneurship and open innovation, leveraging the interdisciplinary approach of STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics).

MISSION
  • Inspire students and researchers to harness their intellectual capabilities to generate new and original ideas.
  • Provide a centralized platform for students and researchers with innovative ideas and creative talents.
  • Make students more curious, imaginative, and creative, making engineering education more engaging.
  • Encourage faculty members to participate in industry interactions and research activities.
  • Establish the IDEA Lab to nurture the creative and entrepreneurial potential among young engineering students, providing seed funding for early start-up exploration from idea to prototype and Minimal Viable Product design.

Our Innovation Journey Begins
IDEA Lab Established — 2026
Officially set up with AICTE approval (AQIS ID: IDEA2024000132) and a total investment of ₹90 Lakhs. Infrastructure commissioned and team onboarded.
Student Registration Drive — In Progress
Enrolling students across all engineering departments as IDEA Lab members. Equipment access, safety briefings, and induction programme are being conducted.
First Workshop Series — Planned Q2 2026
Design thinking and hands-on equipment workshops will be the first milestone activities, introducing students to the maker mindset and STEM application.
First Prototype Development — Target Q3 2026
Student project teams will begin working on their first prototypes with guidance from TechGurus and lab coordinators. Industry problems will be prioritized.
Patent Filing & IP Support — Target 2027
Promising innovations from student and faculty projects will be taken through the IPR pipeline. Consultancy tie-ups for patent support are being explored.
